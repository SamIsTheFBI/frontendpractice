Why this look?

You might ask why I opted for this old styled look. No it's not the lack of knowledge modern web design. And no, those links above won't lead to similar looking webpages. The purpose behind this minimalism was to show that basic HTML/CSS can help you achieve a lot. A website doesn't exactly need to be filled with shiny gradients or that parallax motion.
